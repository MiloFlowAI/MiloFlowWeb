How it works

Talk. Capture. Act. Remember.

A short loop for moving from spoken thought to useful record without managing another system.

  1. 01 Talk

    Voice or text in Milo.

  2. 02 Capture

    Notes, summaries, and receipts stay attached.

  3. 03 Act

    Review reminders and action items.

  4. 04 Remember

    Search local records or export Markdown.

Memory and privacy

Your knowledge base belongs to you.

Unlike a generic chat session, Milo can build continuity around your own notes and preferences. The source of truth is your local app data and optional Markdown vault.

User-owned vault

Keep readable Markdown notes in a folder you choose. Milo can search them when you ask.

Memory over time

Milo can remember stable preferences and past work so future conversations start with context.

Your controls

Refresh notes, delete the index, forget the folder, use Incognito, or export a SQLite backup.
